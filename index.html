Methylated spirit BB gun

Toy BB gun intended to be mounted on a remote control vehicle. Many difficulties have been encountered trying to get proper fuel mixtures and carburation. Better check valves are required before progressing

Light gas combustion gun

Intended to be used as a toy for target shooting and general purpose fun, requires hydrogen generator to function. Utilises hall effect sensors to trigger ignition of gasses after initial compression phase. The main purpose is to test hyper acceleration of BB through ignition of gasses after break of inertia due to general chamber compressor via spring and trigger system

Light array / graphics equaliser display

Background light array intended to have graphic eqauliser display addded at later date. utilising MSQEG7 ic...... After purchasing several FAKE ICs I started on my own. Using LM358 & LM386 ICs I have made a simple 4 channel bandpass filter arrangement using passive and active filtering. It still needs work but is good enough for a basic display.
