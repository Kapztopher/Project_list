Small tracked RC vehicles

These are several variations of design attempts for small RC vehicles, the were semi-successful failed all-in-all. Failure was greatly due to drive design as the tracked devices kept lossing there tracks and the robot-car style devices didn't have proper rear guide wheel or coaster ( due to being produced at extremely low budget and mostly out of scrap found)

Rocker boggie vehicle

This is an attempt at an remote controlled rocker boggie vehicle capable of climbing stairs. Still very much work in progressing due to power delivery issues limiting turnoing capability. I am currently working on a higher amperage buck convertor to power the H-brigge drivers.

Methylated spirit BB gun

Toy BB gun intended to be mounted on a remote control vehicle. Many difficulties have been encountered trying to get proper fuel mixtures and carburation. Better check valves are required before progressing

Light gas combustion gun

Intended to be used as a toy for target shooting and general purpose fun, requires hydrogen generator to function. Utilises hall effect sensors to trigger ignition of gasses after initial compression phase. The main purpose is to test hyper acceleration of BB through ignition of gasses after break of inertia due to general chamber compressor via spring and trigger system
