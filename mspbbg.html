Methylated spirit BB gun

The methylated spirits BB gun, runs of a carburator and two small air pumps. One which supplies clean air, and another which draws fuel mixture through the carburator. It has an automatic, gravity based feed system, which enables control of the amnount of BB's fed in aswell as allowing it to be remote controlled. For the purpose of safe testing it was mounted to a robot car for remote testing.
